// SOCIAL ENGINEERING TACTICS

🎭 Pretexting
Attackers fabricate a scenario to steal info – fake surveys, impersonation of co-workers.
🍯 Baiting
USB drops, free downloads infected with malware, enticing physical media.
📧 Quid Pro Quo
Offering a benefit (gift card, IT help) in exchange for credentials.
🔄 Tailgating
Physical unauthorized access following an employee into secure area.
😈 Deepfake Vishing
AI-generated voice clones for executive impersonation.
