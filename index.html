What is Phishing?

Phishing is a fraudulent attempt to obtain sensitive information by disguising as a trustworthy entity in electronic communications.

How to Recognize Phishing Attempts

  • Look for generic greetings like "Dear Customer".
  • Check the sender's email address for inconsistencies.
  • Be cautious of unsolicited attachments.

What to Do if You're Targeted

If you suspect a phishing attempt, do not respond or click any links; report it to your IT department.
